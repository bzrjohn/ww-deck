The New Playbook

The Andromeda Era.

Meta's advertising platform fundamentally changed in 2024-2025. The old playbook — interest stacking, lookalike audiences, manual bidding — is dead. Andromeda is Meta's new AI-powered ad retrieval engine, and it changes everything.

Old Meta Ads (Pre-Andromeda) New Meta Ads (Andromeda Era)
Targeting-first: you pick audiences Creative-first: AI matches creative to users

2–3 ad variants per ad set 10–20 conceptually distinct creatives per campaign

You find the audience The algorithm finds the audience — you feed it creative

"Your creative IS your targeting now." In the Andromeda era, the algorithm doesn't need you to tell it who to show ads to — it needs you to give it enough creative diversity to figure it out itself. Andromeda processes 15M+ new ad creatives monthly using NVIDIA Grace Hopper Superchips.

Google Ads — Search.

People actively Googling "mortgage broker near me" are ready to go. Google Ads captures high-intent demand that complements Meta's awareness-driven approach.

How Meta + Google Work Together

Meta Ads (Awareness → Interest)

Creates demand. People see your ads, learn about you, build familiarity. They may not need a broker today — but when they do, they remember you.

Google Ads (Intent → Action)

Captures demand. When someone Googles "mortgage broker Adelaide" or "compare home loan rates," your ad appears at the top. They're ready to act.

The Two-Channel Advantage

Meta creates awareness and builds your brand. Google captures the people who are actively searching. Together, they create a full-funnel system where no lead falls through the cracks.

The flywheel effect: Someone sees your Meta ad on Monday, Googles "Apex Finance" on Thursday, and books a consultation. Neither channel works as well alone — together, they compound.
